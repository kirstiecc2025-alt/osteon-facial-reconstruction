Uncertainty statement

This output is a facial approximation, not a portrait or positive identification. Cranial anatomy constrains some proportions, while soft-tissue depth, lips, ears, pigmentation, hair and other surface characteristics remain partly or wholly uncertain. Multiple variants should be considered together.
